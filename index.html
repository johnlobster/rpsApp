Instructions

The object of the game is to defeat all your enemies

First - select your hero - all the others become your enemies

Second - choose the enemy you will face

Third - fight your enemy by clicking on the light sabre


Each time your hero attacks, his ability to attack increases - the enemies attack does not increase

When your enemy is defeated, move on to the next one

When all the enemies are defeated, you can start again
